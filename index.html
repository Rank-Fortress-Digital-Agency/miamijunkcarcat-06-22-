Most insurance companies will charge higher rates for insuring older cars since they’re more likely to be involved in accidents. When you get rid of your junk car, you’re helping reduce harmful emissions that contribute to climate change and pollute our air and water. You’re also doing your part to conserve resources and keep our planet healthy. So if you want to do something good for yourself and the environment, getting rid of your junk car is a great way to start.
